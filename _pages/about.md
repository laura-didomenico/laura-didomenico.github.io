I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Data Science Institute, [Hasselt University](https://www.uhasselt.be/nl/wie-is-wie/detail/laura-didomenico), in Belgium. I am specialized in data-driven modeling of infectious disease dynamics, integrating human behavior through social contacts and mobility and accounting for population socio-economic heterogeneity. I have been working extensively on social contact matrices and real-time modeling of COVID-19 to assess effectiveness of control strategies and inform pandemic response.

Research interests
------
- modeling for outbreak response
  [BMC Med 2020](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/s12916-020-01698-4), [NatComms 2021](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-021-21249-6)
- model-data integration of disease dynamics and human behavior
- impact assessment of NPIs and vaccination strategies
- generalized social contact matrices
  

Education

- Master Degree in Mathematics for Life and Data Sciences, University of Trento, 2019
- Bachelor Degree in Mathematics, University of Trento, 2017
